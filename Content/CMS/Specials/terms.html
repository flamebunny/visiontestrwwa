When you bet with SuperPick, you get to choose from a range of specials including Money Back and Profit plus options. Once a day on any Australian race. Simply pick your race, pick your runner and pick your special.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

GENERAL

  • SuperPick is available to RWWA (TABtouch) account holders transacting via Internet or Mobile wagering platforms only.
  • SuperPick is not available to NSW residents.
  • RWWA (TABtouch) account holders will be allocated a predetermined number of tokens, referred to as a SuperPick, each calendar day (WST).
  • The number of SuperPick tokens issued is at the sole discretion of RWWA.
  • The number of available SuperPick tokens can be sourced by an account holder once their account is in a logged in state.
  • SuperPick tokens may only be activated on Fixed Odds ‘Final Field’ Win + Place markets. Tokens cannot be activated on any other description of Fixed Odds racing market or bet type, including but not limited to Feature Races - All In, Jockey Challenge, Greyhounds Box Challenge, Odds vs Evens and/or Inside vs Outside.
  • A maximum of one SuperPick token may be activated on an individual race.
  • Standard SuperPick tokens:
    • A standard token shall be issued to account holders at 12.00am each calendar day (WST) and expire at 11.59pm the same calendar day (WST).
    • A standard token does not accrue if not activated within the prescribed calendar day (WST).
    • A standard token may be activated on any Fixed Odds ‘Final Field’ racing market only where SuperPick options are offered.
    • A standard token is activated by the account holder at the time of bet placement to identify the transaction is applicable to a SuperPick and the SuperPick option that has been selected.
    • Additional standard token(s) may be allocated to account holders from time to time at the sole discretion of RWWA.
    • A standard token shall only be reissued to the account holder if the selected runner is scratched or the race is abandoned in the same calendar day (WST) as the standard token was activated. In such circumstances the standard token will be reissued to the account holder at the time the Fixed Odds market containing the scratched runner is settled or the Fixed Odds market is abandoned.
  • Bonus SuperPick tokens:
    • A bonus token(s) may be allocated to account holders from time to time at the discretion of RWWA.
    • A bonus token may only be activated on the specific racing code, race meeting and/or race as stipulated by RWWA.
    • A bonus token may be activated at any time until the race(s) or meeting(s) applicable to the bonus token closes for betting.
    • A bonus token is activated by an account holder at the time of bet placement to identify the transaction is applicable to a SuperPick and the SuperPick option that has been selected.
    • A bonus token shall automatically be utilised in advance of a standard token.
    • A bonus token, if specific to an individual race, will not be reinstated to the account holder if the selected runner is scratched or the race is abandoned.
    • A bonus token, if specific to a meeting, shall be reinstated to the account holder if a selected runner is scratched. In such circumstances the bonus token shall reissued to the account holder at the time the Fixed Odds market containing the scratched runner is settled. The only exception is where the bonus token was activated by the account holder on the final race of the meeting, in which case the bonus token shall not be reissued.
  • SuperPick is available on selected Thoroughbred, Harness and Greyhound races at the sole discretion of RWWA.
  • The value associated and the number of SuperPick options offered on any one race at any point in time is at the sole discretion of RWWA.
  • SuperPick is available for Fixed Odds ‘Singles’ bets only. Fixed Odds ‘Multi’ and/or Parimutuel wagers are ineligible.
  • Applicable bets shall have details pertaining to the SuperPick option selected referenced on transaction receipt.
  • Any promotional dividend due resulting from a successful SuperPick shall be credited to the customer’s account.
  • Any promotional dividend due resulting from a successful SuperPick shall be paid coinciding with the release of all Fixed Odds dividends on the nominated race.
  • All bets applicable to a SuperPick shall be subject to all relevant and existing Fixed Odds Rules.
  • Any change to the unofficial finishing positions in the nominated race as a result of a successful protest shall be taken into account.
  • Any amendments made to the official placings in the nominated race post declaration of correct weight shall not be taken into account.
  • SuperPick is intended for recreational gamblers only. Professional gamblers and customers that abuse the spirit of the promotion as deemed by RWWA will not be eligible for promotional dividends.
  • RWWA reserves the right to exclude nominated wagers, at its sole discretion, from SuperPick options without prior notification.
  • RWWA reserves the right to terminate or alter any or all SuperPick options and the events applicable at any time without prior notification.

SUPERPICK OPTIONS

  • Money Back - Run 2nd; Run 2nd or 3rd; Run 2nd, 3rd or 4th
    • Applicable bets placed on the runner(s) that is officially declared as finishing in the position(s) specified in the SuperPick option shall receive a promotional dividend, equal to the Win stake invested, up to the maximum amount outlined.
    • Applicable bets are deemed as Fixed Odds ‘Win’ and the win component of Fixed Odds ‘Win & Place’ bets where the account holder has activated a relevant SuperPick token.
    • Any dead heat(s) shall be taken into account, however any promotional dividend paid shall not be affected by the number of runners that have dead heated.
  • Money Back - Run 4th (Place)
    • Applicable bets placed on the runner(s) that is officially declared as finishing in fourth position shall receive a promotional dividend, equal to the Place stake invested, up to the maximum amount outlined.
    • Applicable bets are deemed as Fixed Odds ‘Place’ and the place component of Fixed Odds ‘Win & Place’ bets where the account holder has activated a relevant SuperPick token.
    • Any dead heat(s) shall be taken into account, however any promotional dividend paid shall not be affected by the number of runners that have dead heated.
  • Profit Plus - x % (Win)
    • Applicable bets placed on the runner that is officially declared the winner of the race shall receive an additional promotional dividend based upon the profit component of the wager.
    • The promotional dividend payable shall be in accordance with the percentage increase specified in the SuperPick option, up to the maximum amount outlined.
    • The profit component of a wager is the actual payout amount not including the stake.
    • Applicable bets are deemed as Fixed Odds ‘Win Only’ bets where the account holder has activated a relevant SuperPick token.
    • ‘Win & Place’, ‘Each Way’, and/or ‘Place Only’ Singles bets are deemed ineligible.
    • In the event of deductions or a dead heat where the initial profit component associated with the wager is reduced, the promotional dividend for successful applicable bets shall be based upon the revised amount.
    • In the event of a dead heat(s), applicable bets placed on all runners that have dead heated shall be entitled to a promotional dividend.
  • Profit Plus - x % (Place)
    • Applicable bets placed on the runners that are officially declared a place getter in the nominated race, in accordance with the number of Fixed Odds placings payable at time of bet placement, shall receive an additional promotional dividend based upon the profit component of the wager. The only exception is where a field is reduced to 4 or less runners. In such circumstances the place book for the event shall be abandoned with all bets and associated SuperPick tokens declared void.
    • The promotional dividend payable shall be in accordance with the percentage increase specified in the SuperPick option, up to the maximum amount outlined.
    • The profit component of a wager is the actual payout amount not including the stake.
    • Applicable bets are deemed as Fixed Odds ‘Place Only’ bets where the account holder has activated a relevant SuperPick token.
    • ‘Win & Place’, ‘Each Way’, and/or ‘Win Only’ Singles bets are deemed ineligible.
    • In the event of deductions or a dead heat where the initial profit component associated with the wager is reduced, the promotional dividend for successful applicable bets shall be based upon the revised amount.
    • In the event of a dead heat(s), applicable bets placed on all runners that have dead heated shall be entitled to a promotional dividend.
  • Pay 4th (Place)
    • Applicable bets placed on the runner(s) that is officially declared as finishing in fourth position shall receive a promotional dividend equal to the projected Place payout associated with the wager, up to the maximum amount specified in the SuperPick option.
    • Applicable bets are deemed as Fixed Odds ‘Place’ and the place component of Fixed Odds ‘Win & Place’ bets where the account holder has activated a relevant SuperPick token.
    • In the event of a dead heat for fourth place, applicable bets placed on all runners that have dead heated shall be entitled to a promotional dividend. In such instances payouts shall be revised in accordance with standard dead heat rules.
